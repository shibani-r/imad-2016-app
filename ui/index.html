The Blogger

Hello! Welcome to my blog! I'm a native of Kerala. I did my graduation in B.Tech(CSE) from NCERC, Thrissur. My pastime favourites include drawing, listening to music, playing badminton and watching animated movies. I love to explore things and this blog was created as a part of that.

Personally, i believe there are certain things one must have in his or her life to make it more valuable and joyous. A lot of us live our lives all maxed out in the hype of the moment that we have forgotten to care about ourselves, and other people as well. But that's not exactly living life to the fullest. The articles here may help you remind the tiny little things you've missed out in your busy life which actually matter a lot. Have a good time reading them! Stay happy and live long!
